The Project

Forged Alliance Forever is a community-driven project designed to facilitate online play for Supreme Commander: Forged Alliance. Complete with chat, matchmaking, replay vaults, tournaments and ever evolving balance. If you want to experience Forged Alliance in its true glory together with thousands of other players of all skill levels, FAF is the place to be.

Multiplayer

Host and join teamgames or measure your skill against others in one versus one combat against players from all over the world.

Modding

Experience countless variations on gameplay and thousands of player made maps to keep things fresh forever.

Community

Meet one of the friendliest online communities out there complete with forums, a voice chat server, clans, wiki and a supremely moderated global chat to enjoy between games.

Non-Profit

FAF is completely free and supported entirely by the community, even cash prize tournaments have no signup fees.
