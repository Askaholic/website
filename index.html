- The Project -

Forged Alliance Forever is a community-driven project designed to facilitate online play for Supreme Commander: Forged Alliance. Complete with game lobby, chat, matchmaking, replay vaults, mod vaults, tournaments and much, much more. If you want to experience Forged Alliance in its true glory together with thousands of other players of all skill levels, FAF is the place to be. Thanks to continued balancing and refinement of gameplay, by the community for the community, you will not find a better place to enjoy this amazing game.





Multiplayer

Non-Profit

FAF is completely free and supported entirely by the community, even cash prize tournaments have no signup fees.

Community

Modding

- Supreme Commander: Forged Alliance -


SupCom FA is the spiritual successor to Total Annihilation. It's a real time strategy game with several unique features that sets it apart from every other generic RTS. Among these features some of the most prominent are:

  • The flux economy; all resource gains and expenditures happen per second rather than consuming a fixed amount of resources immediatly, allowing for unparalleled flexibility.
  • Time itself is also a resource as moving around buildpower allows you to complete projects as fast as your economy will allow.
  • A scale that dwarfs even modern strategy games; most RTS games function on a scale similar to a battle, here you are fighting a war.
  • Strategic zoom and other UI features that let you give complex commands, ferry routes, infinite build ques and much more with extreme ease.
  • Real time physics simulation; projectiles can be dodged, stray shells can hit unsuspecting targets and many more simulated effects.
  • Reclaim; destroyed units leave behind wrecks, no longer are you only fighting to damage the enemy, but also to secure the mass left behind by destroyed units.
